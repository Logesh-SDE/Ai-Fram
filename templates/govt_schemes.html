How to Apply

  1. Select the scheme relevant to your farming needs
  2. Check eligibility criteria carefully
  3. Prepare required documents (Aadhaar, land records, bank details)
  4. Visit the official website or nearest agriculture office
  5. Fill application form with accurate information
  6. Submit supporting documents
  7. Track application status regularly

Common Documents Required

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Land ownership documents
  • Bank account details
  • Passport size photographs
  • Caste certificate (if applicable)
  • Income certificate (for certain schemes)
